WELCOME TO INGREDIENTS

  • 400 gr of spaghetti. A portion can be between 80 and 120 gr per person, depending on taste and if it is a single dish or not in your menu.
  • 300 gr of minced meat (100 gr of lean pork and 200 gr of veal stifle). The best minced meat is the one that you choose in the butcher's shop and they mince it to you at the moment, forget the ones that are already packaged.
  • 1 carrot.
  • 1 onion.
  • 100 ml of white wine.
  • 200 gr of tomato sauce or fried tomato. But if you like it with more proportion of sauce you can even double the quantity of tomato sauce.
  • 50 ml of milk.
  • Oregano.
  • Some basil leaves for decoration.
  • Olive oil, salt and freshly ground black pepper.
